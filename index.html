About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio! I am a passionate researcher exploring the fascinating intersection of mathematics and computer science. My work focuses on algebraic structures, formal methods, and theoretical computer science, with a particular emphasis on the beautiful connections between algebra and logic in computational systems. I believe that understanding these fundamental relationships is key to advancing both fields.
